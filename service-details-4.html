Terrazzo Works

Terrazzo is a timeless, durable, and highly customizable flooring solution made from natural stone aggregates (marble, quartz, granite) or recycled glass combined with cement or epoxy binders. At Neziah Contractors, we deliver premium terrazzo installations for residential, commercial, and institutional spaces, offering seamless, polished surfaces that combine elegance with exceptional longevity.

Our terrazzo works are designed to withstand heavy traffic while maintaining their beauty for decades. We offer both traditional cement-based terrazzo and modern epoxy terrazzo systems, allowing for endless design possibilities including custom patterns, colors, and logo inlays. With expert craftsmanship and attention to detail, we ensure flawless execution from substrate preparation to final polishing.

Service Benefits

Terrazzo flooring provides unmatched durability, aesthetic versatility, and low long-term maintenance costs. It is ideal for high-traffic areas and adds significant value to any property through its premium appearance and lifespan.

  • Extremely durable and long-lasting surface
  • Custom colors, patterns, and aggregate options
  • Low maintenance – easy to clean and refinish
  • Cost-effective over the life of the installation
  • -  Resistant to stains, scratches, and heavy foot traffic
  • -  Seamless and hygienic – no grout lines
  • -  Timeless aesthetic that never goes out of style
  • -  Eco-friendly options using recycled materials
  • -  Increases property value with premium finish

Have You Any Question

  • What materials are used in terrazzo flooring?

    Terrazzo consists of natural aggregates such as marble, quartz, granite, or recycled glass chips mixed with either a cement or epoxy binder. The surface is then ground and polished to create a smooth, durable finish.

  • How long does terrazzo flooring last?

    With proper installation and occasional maintenance, terrazzo can last 40–75 years or more. It is one of the most long-lasting flooring options available and can be restored to like-new condition through re-polishing.

  • Is terrazzo suitable for high-traffic areas?

    Yes — terrazzo is excellent for high-traffic commercial and public spaces such as airports, hospitals, schools, hotels, and shopping malls due to its exceptional durability and resistance to wear.

  • How do you maintain terrazzo floors?

    Maintenance is simple: regular sweeping or dust mopping, damp mopping with neutral cleaner, and periodic polishing/sealing. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive pads to preserve the finish.
